So what happens now?

  • Now that the booking has been confirmed, the client will be charged for the full booking 48 hours prior to the start. CareVine will hold your payment in escrow until the booking has been canceled or completed. Once the booking is complete, all applicable funds will be transferred to the you.
  • After the booking is complete, the client will have 48 hours to submit any disputes if issues arise.
  • This booking will repeat weekly. Please make sure to check the booking for any exceptions to the schedule.
  • You can cancel the booking at any time. Any booking time not completed will be fully refunded to the client. Your search ranking may also be impacted.
