How do disputes work?

  • If you experienced any issues with your booking, you can submit a dispute by navigating to My Bookings and finding the booking you want to dispute. Click the Dispute button and submit a description of the issue.
  • Once submitted, the CareVine team will review your dispute and determine the next steps. Once we come to a decision, you will be notified of the outcome and refunded the decided amount, if any.
  • The caregiver will also be asked to provide input on the situation if necessary.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact CareVine Support.
